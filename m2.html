Music explains fundamental principles of relation.
Beauty is the product of harmony, tonality and rhythm.
Harmony exists at intervals and between scales.
Looking at the world, harmony and disharmony are both apparent.
Different combinations produce different results.
Dissonance is a symbol of dissenting opinion.
It can be a highly impactful element in the right application,
as can be a dissenting opinion in a room full of toadies.
Tonality reveals that harmony between scales alone is not sufficient to enchant.
Each note represents a choice; a collection of notes can portray a lifetime of choices.
Like words, notes detail the character of our expression
and are most potent at their most meaningful.
