Satisfy the muse, the angst, the hunger, the soul, the mind,
but never seek to satisfy an audience.
If the music is impactful, satisfaction will sort itself out.
Music has soothed many a savage beast and will continue to do so,
but it must address something other than his boredom.
Entertainment rarely inspires, expands the mind or empowers.
Entertainment pacifies. Music does everything else.
