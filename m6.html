You do not control the creative process.
The part of us which controls is not the part of us that creates.
Allow for chance, randomness, flexibility, and even imperfection.
These are the handmaidens of creation. Make yourself available to the forces.
Music is not for ego-satisfaction; that is a parasitic relationship.
An irresponsible use of power. A gardener cannot force a plant to grow.
He can merely produce the conditions in which plant growth is likely to occur.
He leaves much to chance. Music is no different. Cultivate, sow and reap.
One cannot simply reap and find anything but a malnourished sort of Music.
It has none of the essential vitamins and minerals we all love,
and we are contracting AudioScurvy en masse.
Turn the soil and plenty healthy crops for next season.
Take a chance on no-control and receive aid from invisible partners.
Control-mind can only sacrifice impact for convention.
Create that which has a will to be born.
