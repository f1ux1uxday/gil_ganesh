For Music to be properly exploited, it must be categorized.
Genre serves this purpose brilliantly, conning musicians into censoring their output
so as to not offend their set boundaries, to appeal to an already conquered marketplace.
This has led to a social phenomenon referred to as Genre People,
monochromatic individuals that behave and dress in manners bound by their genre preferences,
as opposed to the liberties of Full Spectrum Audio,
which defies petty categorizations and transcends false boundaries.

Punks, gangsters, hicks, the elderly, the mall rats, the hippies, the hepcats, the club kids
are all turf-based and genre-bound street gangs.
We wade around in stagnant water when we prevent our currents from mixing.
The same decay occurs in songs that convey solitary and predictable emotions:
Atrophy.
